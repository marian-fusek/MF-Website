BIO

To be real with you — I don't do any of this out of pure passion. My process is a battle. With the brief, the obvious, and whatever comes too easy. That restlessness — that's where everything I do comes from. Design, leadership, coaching. All of it.

Under the performance, the noise, and the safe choices — there's something worth finding. When those layers finally break, the real and beautiful comes alive.

The brief doesn't own the outcome. The work answers to your vision — and comes out MF-stamped.

Having fun drawing rectangles or giving ten tips for a better life might work for some. But if you just need something raw, let's talk.

My words, not AI. Been em-dashing since before it was a red flag.

I'm open to freelance projects and jobs in design, leadership and coaching. Would enjoy even a combo of all these together. Or even doing nothing and get paid – I got things to create for myself.

Also, please, dear human, approach me if you appreciate that weird playful bitterness that I push in between the lines all over this website. If you need a quiet designer number 13, who will manage an established design system and do research to push page conversions from 3.1% to 5%, wrong address.

In design I like brand systems, but also small projects, visual refreshes, consultations and reviews. When it comes to leadership, I like to focus on people development, team processes, establishing team culture in a start up environment. Surprise me! Lastly, coaching is sewn into everything I do. I have a proven track of being the guy people go to when they really need help. Be it design, leadership or a personal issue, I can be there for them.

I work remote but am open to relocation – extra interest in New Zealand, South Korea, Japan and EU Nordic countries.

CCO / Creative at MIUNĀE – Skincare.

I designed the first brand idea that over half a year grew into a trademarked logo and symbol – approved, manufactured and sold bottles and boxes. Complete Instagram presence, print materials, marketing videos, AI production... the list goes on. All on brand. Majority of all this in tandem with Linda Krestanova – CBO / Brand.

Currently exploring and working from Da Nang – Vietnam.
