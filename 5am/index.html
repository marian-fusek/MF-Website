01 — What We Do

Services

We start with the one true thing about a brand — the thing that actually separates it — and build outward from there. Not five values on a slide. One truth, dressed in a system.

  • 01 Brand Identity Marks, systems, the whole visual language — built to survive contact with reality, not just a pitch deck.
  • 02 Brand Voice & Copywriting The words underneath the words. Tone, taglines, the sentence that gets stuck in someone's head.
  • 03 Creative Direction Photo, video, campaigns — one point of view carried across everything a brand puts into the world.
  • 04 Digital Design Interfaces and sites that don't perform minimalism — they practice it.
  • 05 Naming & Messaging What to call it, and what to say about it — decided at the same table, not in two different departments.
No decks. No buzzwords. Just the work.
02 — Selected Work

Projects

MIUNĀE

Brand Identity — Creative Direction — Digital
Context

A Romanian botanical skincare brand, built from zero — no name, no shelf, no story yet.

Approach

We built the whole system around one idea: time. The hourglass became the mark itself, replacing the I in the wordmark. Everything else — palette, packaging, the site — followed from that one decision.

Scope

Brand identity, packaging direction, art direction for photo and video, and the site itself.

GoBaller

Product Design — iOS — Brand
Context

An iOS app for people who organize pickup basketball — built for the fifteen minutes before anyone actually plays.

Approach

Speed over ceremony. Every screen answers one question and gets out of the way — who's playing, where, and when.

Scope

Product design, interaction design, app icon and identity.
